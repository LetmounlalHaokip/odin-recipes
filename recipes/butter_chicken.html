Butter Chicken

Description

A rich and creamy Indian dish featuring tender chicken pieces cooked in a flavorful tomato-based gravy with butter and aromatic spices.

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into cubes
  • 2 tbsp butter + 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 cup tomato puree (or blended fresh tomatoes)
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste, 1 tsp garam masala, 1 tsp red chili powder, 1/2 tsp turmeric

Steps to Cook

  1. Marinate Chicken: Mix chicken with 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste, 1/2 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp red chili powder, and salt. Let it sit for 20 mins.
  2. Cook Chicken: Heat 1 tbsp oil, sautÃ© chicken until lightly browned. Remove and set aside.
  3. Prepare Gravy: In the same pan, melt butter. Add 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste, sautÃ© for 30 secs. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  4. Combine & Simmer: Add 1 tsp garam masala, salt, and the cooked chicken. Stir in fresh cream and simmer for 5-7 mins.
  5. Finish & Serve: Garnish with coriander leaves and a drizzle of cream. Serve hot with naan or rice.
